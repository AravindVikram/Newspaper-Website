Top 10 best-selling SUVs in FY23. TATA and Hyundai takes up the mantle.


Fiscal Year 2023 was a record year for the car industry with volumes of 3,889,545 units, beating the previous best of 3,377,436 units achieved in FY19. The largest contribution came from the SUV segment, which garnered sales of 1,673,488 units. Among the 10 best-selling SUVs in FY23 were two models each from Tata Motors (Nexon and Punch), Hyundai Motor India (Creta and Venue) and Kia India (Seltos and Sonet), one from Maruti Suzuki India (Brezza) and an impressive three from Mahindra & Mahindra (Bolero, Scorpio and XUV700). The Tata Punch continued to throw a heavy punch as it closed FY23 with handsome domestic volumes of 133,819 units.
The popularity of the Hyundai Venue keeps growing as it registered wholesale despatches of 120,653 units. Mahindra's mainstay, the Mahindra Bolero, once again proved that it is the numero uno brand of the company clocking sales of 100,577 units. While the Kia Seltos recorded sales of 100,132 units, the Kia Sonet was quite far behind its rivals at 94,096 units. The Mahindra Scorpio ended the fiscal at 76,935 units.High on space, comfort, practicality, power and utility. SUVs are the first choice for buyers looking for a car that caters to the entire family. While compact SUVs are great when it comes to mostly city usage, it is the mid-size and full-size SUVs that offer performance that can take on any road. This is the reason behind the ever-increasing popularity of SUVs that register impressive sales figures in the month of February 2023.

Top 10 best-selling SUVs in FY23:

Tata Nexon - 172,138 units

Hyundai Creta - 150,372 units

Maruti Suzuki Brezza - 145,665 units

Tata Punch - 133,819 units

Hyundai Venue - 120,653 units

Mahindra Bolero - 100,577 units

Kia Seltos - 100,132 units

Kia Sonet - 94,096 units

Mahindra Scorpio - 76,935 units

Mahindra XUV700 - 66,473 units
